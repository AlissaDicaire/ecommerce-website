All About Bee's Candles

Our candles are made in Canada and are 100% all natural. Not only are they made in our native country but they are made from the finest 100% bees wax, also from Canada. The wax used comes from local bee farmers and helps support local growth and income. When you buy from Bee's Candles, you are supporting your local community and helping to spread awareness for future buyers. When you buy from Bee's Candles, you will get a card that is attached telling you where the candle was made.

More About Bee's Wax!

Bee's wax candles are better for your health too. Studies show that candles that are made from man made waxes use harsh chemicals, that when we breath in, are not healthy. When you burn bee's wax, its 100% natural and therefore your home is not being filled with toxic fumes.

Bee's wax candles also burn longer. So not only are they better for your physical health, but they are longer lasting and therefore you to enjoy longer. You can buy them all different shapes and sizes, as well as honey comb shape or melted textures. The larger ones do burn longer than the smaller candles but they do burn at the same rate.
