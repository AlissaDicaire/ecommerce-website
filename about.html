Difference between Beeswax and Man Made Wax

Beeswax Candles

Our Beeswax candles are Made in Canada and are 100% all natural. Not only are they made in our native country but they are made from the finest 100% bees wax, also from Canada. The wax used comes from local bee farmers and helps support local growth and income. When you buy from Bee's Candles, you are supporting your local community and helping to spread awareness for future buyers. When you buy from Bee's Candles, you will get a card that is attached telling you where the candle was made.

Bee's wax candles are better for your health too. Studies show that candles that are made from man made waxes use harsh chemicals, that when we breath in, are not healthy. When you burn bee's wax, its 100% natural and therefore your home is not being filled with toxic fumes.

Bee's wax candles also burn longer. So not only are they better for your physical health, but they are longer lasting and there for you to enjoy longer. You can buy them all different shapes and sizes, as well as in honey comb textures or smooth textures. The larger ones do burn longer than the smaller candles but they do burn at the same rate.

Some other facts about Beeswax are:
  • Not tested on animals
  • Solvent free
  • Non-carcinogenic
  • Does not contain hazardous additives such as naphtha or naphthalene
  • Natural Colour

Man Made Wax Candles

Unlike bee's wax candles, man made wax candles are cheaper (up to 9 times cheaper) and come in a variety of different scents. Our wax candles also are made in Canada but come from an entirely different process. The candles from Bee's candles are made from paraffin wax and other plant waxes. Since regular wax candles are more easily accessable, they can come in more of a variety or scents, colours and forms. Regular wax candles from Bee's Candles come in many different scents but most popular are:

  • Pine
  • Vanilla
  • Mandarin cranberry
  • Cinnamon
  • Olive flower

They also come in many different shapes and sizes, as well as function and decoration. Pick from simple tea light candles, to more decorative and large scented candles. Tall and thin candles are perfect for a little light at the dinner table! Or if you're in the mood for a little R & R, light a nice scented Lavendar candle. Each scent provides a specific aroma to fit every day events.
